name   :=       sbt201

* The types of Keys and it's purposes:
1. SettingKey[T] - Computed Once per project load
2. TaskKey[T] - Recomputed every time called
3. InputKet[T] - Accepts Command-line arguments

NOTES: A TASK can depend on SETTINGKey.
       A SETTINGKey can depend on SETTINGKey.
       BUT a SETTINGKEY cannot depend on TASKKEY.
